Frequently asked questions

Is QuickSplit free?

Yes. Core features — splitting expenses, balances, UPI settle-up, receipt scanning, and the AI assistant — are free to use during early access.

How does UPI settle-up work?

QuickSplit generates a standard UPI payment link and QR code for the amount you owe. You pay from your own UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and others). QuickSplit never holds or moves your money — it just records that the balance is settled.

How does receipt scanning work?

Snap a photo of a bill and QuickSplit reads the total so you can split it. You can always adjust the detected amounts before saving.

Can I move my data from Splitwise?

Yes — you can import existing groups and expenses from a Splitwise CSV export so you don't have to start over.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Account → Settings → Delete account, or email hello@quicksplit.app from your registered address and we'll remove your account and personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details on retention.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email hello@quicksplit.app with the details. Bug reports with steps to reproduce and a screenshot help us fix things fastest.
