Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

QuickSplit ("QuickSplit", "we", "us") is an expense-splitting app that helps you track shared expenses, settle balances over UPI, scan receipts, and understand your spending. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. By using QuickSplit you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address and/or phone number, and password (stored only as a secure hash) when you register.
  • Expense and group data — the expenses, groups, friends, balances, settlements, comments, and chat messages you create in the app.
  • Payment references — your UPI ID (VPA) when you choose to generate UPI payment links or QR codes. We do not process payments or store bank or card details; settlement happens inside your own UPI app.
  • Receipt images — photos you scan are processed to extract amounts so you can split them. You control which images you upload.
  • AI assistant messages — questions you send to the in-app finance assistant, along with the relevant expense context needed to answer them.
  • Technical data — basic device and log information (such as app version and error logs) used to keep the service running and secure.

How we use your information

  • To provide core features: splitting expenses, calculating balances, and generating UPI links and QR codes.
  • To power receipt scanning and the AI finance assistant.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose problems, improve reliability, and develop new features.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only:

  • With people you choose — friends and group members see the shared expenses, balances, and messages you add to a group.
  • With service providers — infrastructure, database, and AI providers that process data on our behalf to operate the app. AI questions may be sent to a third-party model provider to generate a response.
  • When required by law — to comply with a valid legal request or to protect our rights and users.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, after which we delete or anonymise your personal data, except where we must retain it to comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data. To make a request, email us at privacy@quicksplit.app.

Security

We use industry-standard measures — encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls — to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work continually to safeguard your information.

Children

QuickSplit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@quicksplit.app.
